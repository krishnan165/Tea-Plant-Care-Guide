Tea Plant Care Guide

Soil for Tea Plants

Well-drained, acidic soil.

Tea plants thrive in well-drained and acidic soil. Ensure good drainage for optimal root health.

Watering

Regular and moderate watering.

Water tea plants regularly, keeping the soil consistently moist but not waterlogged.

Sunlight

Filtered sunlight.

Provide filtered sunlight to tea plants, avoiding direct, intense sunlight for extended periods.

Benefits

Homegrown tea leaves.

Growing tea plants at home allows you to harvest your own tea leaves for a personalized tea experience.

Greenery and relaxation.

Tea plants add a touch of greenery to your space and contribute to a relaxing environment.

Considerations

Protect from cold drafts; watch for pests such as mites and aphids.
